About Stocked

Stocked is a simple, intuitive pantry management app designed to help you stay on top of what you have, what's expiring, and what to cook. No more forgotten cans in the back of the cupboard or spoiled milk - just a well-stocked kitchen.

How it works

  • Add items manually
  • Scan receipt: Receipt scanning uses OCR and may take a few seconds to process. You'll need to grant access to your camera.
  • Track expirations
  • Stay organized
  • Plan ahead

Need Help?

  • Tap the Edit button next to any item to edit it.
  • Use the remote button to delete an item.
  • Add item at the bottom of "My Panty screen.
